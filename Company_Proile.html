Company Profile

Experience

TC SurveysPty Ltd was founded in 1999 by Greg West. Greg had acquired a broad range of experience in all facets of surveying over 12 years, working as a surveyor at a major engineering consultancy company in Perth. Using this experience he was able to successfully operate and grow his business to what it is today, a boutique surveying firm, specialising in high-rise and major infrastructure project construction.
